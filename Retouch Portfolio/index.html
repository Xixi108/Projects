About Me

Hi, I'm Nikki — a passionate photo retoucher with an eye for detail and a love for visual storytelling. I specialize in enhancing portraits, product photos, and lifestyle images: removing blemishes, adjusting lighting and color, and bringing out the best in every shot without losing authenticity.

Whether you need subtle edits or full transformations, I'm here to help your photos shine.

✦ Services I offer

  • Skin smoothing & blemish removal
  • Color correction & lighting
  • Background cleanup or replacement
  • Object removal
  • Fashion & editorial retouching
  • Photo restoration
  • Real estate photo editing
  • Product & lifestyle editing

Let's make your images look their absolute best. Feel free to reach out — I'd love to work with you!
