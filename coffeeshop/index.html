Why Manila?

Manila's Coffee Scene is Thriving

Manila has emerged as Southeast Asia's specialty coffee capital, with world-class baristas, third-wave roasteries, and unique local flavors. From historic Escolta to modern BGC, you'll find meticulously crafted brews at every turn.

  • 🌍 Single-origin beans from Philippine highlands
  • ☕ Expert latte art and pour-over techniques
  • 💼 Perfect workspaces for remote professionals
  • 🎨 Instagram-worthy aesthetic café designs

Specialty Coffee

The Art of Pour Over Coffee

Pour over is Manila's signature specialty brew — hot water poured slowly over fresh grounds, producing a clean, bright cup that reveals a bean's true character. No pressure, no shortcuts, just precision and patience.

  • ✦ Full flavor clarity — every fruity, floral, and chocolatey note comes through
  • ✦ Best with single-origin beans — taste the story of where it was grown
  • ✦ A ritual worth watching — the bloom, the slow pours, the patience
  • ✦ 6 top Manila spots — from Yardstick in Makati to The Den in Escolta
