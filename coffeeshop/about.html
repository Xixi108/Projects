Who We Are

About Brew Haven

A coffee blog born out of a love for Manila's specialty coffee scene — and a mission to make great coffee accessible to everyone.

Our Story

Started Over a Cup of Coffee

Brew Haven started as a simple idea: Manila has incredible coffee, and not enough people know about it. We wanted to change that.

We visit cafés, talk to baristas, taste the brews, and write about what we find — honestly and without the jargon. Whether you're a casual coffee drinker or a growing enthusiast, our goal is to help you enjoy every cup a little more.

From the historic alleyways of Escolta to the modern streets of BGC, we explore Manila's coffee culture one shop at a time.
