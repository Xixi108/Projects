The Den: Award-Winning Specialty Roastery in Historic Escolta

An intimate look at Manila's most celebrated specialty coffee roastery. Discover their single-origin beans, art-filled space, and what makes their coffee exceptional.

First Impressions

Walking into The Den feels like stepping into an art gallery meets specialty coffee haven. Located in the heart of historic Escolta, this award-winning roastery has earned its reputation as one of Manila's finest coffee establishments. The space is intimate, thoughtfully designed, and filled with local artwork that changes regularly.

The Coffee

The Den specializes in single-origin coffees, sourced directly from coffee farmers across the Philippines and internationally. Each bean is carefully selected and roasted in-house to bring out unique flavor profiles. The baristas here are knowledgeable and passionate, readily discussing the origin story and tasting notes of each coffee.

Whether you opt for their signature espresso drink or a pour-over, you'll taste the difference that quality and expertise make. The coffee is bold, complex, and never bitter—a testament to their roasting skill.

Atmosphere & Community

Beyond the coffee, The Den has cultivated something special—a community. You'll often find regulars camped at tables with their laptops, artists discussing their work, and coffee enthusiasts engaging in passionate conversations about brewing methods. The Den isn't just a café; it's a gathering place for Manila's creative and coffee-loving community.

Rating: ★★★★★ (4.8/5)

📍 Location: Escolta, Manila

📞 Phone: (02) 8241-3538

💰 Price Range: ₱150-300 per drink

The Den deserves its reputation as one of Manila's best coffee shops. If you're serious about specialty coffee, this is essential visit.
